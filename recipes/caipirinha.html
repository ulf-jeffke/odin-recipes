Caipirinha

Description

The caipirinha comes from Brazil and is also considered the secret national drink there.

Cachaca is a Brazilian liquor made from sugar cane must.

With us, the cocktail is often simply referred to as a caipi. There are countless variations worldwide, for example a Caipirinha with vodka is called Caipiroska, with Aperol it is called Caipirol and with rice wine Caipisake.

Ingredients

  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 ounces cachaça
  • 2 teaspoon crushed ice
  • Garnish: lime wheel

Steps

  1. In a double rocks glass, muddle the lime wedges and sugar
  2. Fill the glass with ice, add the cachaça, and stir briefly
  3. Garnish with a lime wheel
